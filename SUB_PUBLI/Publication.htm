Publication

Each accepted submission must be presented at the conference as oral or poster presentations for its publication. Accepted articles will be published by Springer Nature in the following two ways.

  • We plan to edit a special issue entitled "Recent developments in Sensing and Imaging" in the journal Sensing and Imaging of Springer & Nature. Sensing and Imaging is in the ESCI and EI among others. The Program Committee will recommend 10 - 20 submissions presented at ICSI 2024 for possible publication in this special issue. Each manuscript must be a full-length regular paper with a significant extension from its conference version. All submissions to the special issue will be subject to the regular reviewing process according to the journal's standard. More information about this special issue will be sent to the relevant authors after the conference.
