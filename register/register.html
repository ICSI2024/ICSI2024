REGISTRATION

About Registration

The registration fee is divided into two categories: student and non-student:

  • Non-student registration fee is RMB 1,200 before August 9 and RMB 1,500 after August 9 and on-site registration;
  • Student registration fee is RMB 800 for registration before August 9, and RMB 1,000 for on-site registration after August 9. Students must be full-time students at the time of registration and will be required to present their student ID cards for on-site registration;
  • Post-doctoral fellows, laboratory technicians, etc. are not included in the student category.


About the registration fee invoice

The conference can issue Beijing VAT ordinary/special invoice (electronic):

  • Each participant must fill in your instutional name, taxpayer identification number and e-mail address correctly. Please note that each invoice cannot be modified after it is issued;
  • Please keep your cell phone online and email address active for timely contact;
  • Registration fee invoices will be issued in the order of payment in the month of the conference and the following month and sent to your e-mail address.


Cancellation and Refund Policy

If you are unable to attend for any reason, please call 15010258570 for a full refund of your registration fee before the conference.

注册须知

注册类型分为两类:学生与非学生类型:

  • 非学生报名费在 8 月 9 日之前为人民币 1,200 元，8 月 9 日之后为人民币 1,500 元，现场报名费为人民币 1,500 元；
