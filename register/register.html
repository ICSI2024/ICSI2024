REGISTRATION

About Registration

The registration fee is divided into two categories: employees and students:

  • The registration fee for employees is RMB 1,200 before August 9 and RMB 1,500 after August 9 and on-site registration;
  • Student registration fee is RMB 800 for registration before August 9, and RMB 1,000 for on-site registration after August 9;
  • To enjoy the discounted registration fee, students must be full-time students at the time of registration and will be required to present their student ID cards for on-site registration.
  • Post-doctoral fellows, laboratory technicians, etc. are not included in the student category.


About the registration fee invoice

The conference can issue Beijing VAT ordinary/special invoice (electronic):

  • Attendees are requested to fill in the unit name, taxpayer identification number and e-mail address correctly, as invoices will not be refunded or exchanged;
  • Please keep your cell phone and email address free for timely contact;
  • Registration fee invoices will be issued in the order of payment in the month of the conference and the following month and sent to the e-mail address.
